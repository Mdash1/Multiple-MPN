## 3. Business Requirement Details

### Business Requirement Summary

At **RPL Dahej**, dispatch planning currently supports only one MPN per vehicle. The requirement is to support **multiple MPNs** for multiple products and warehouses under a single vehicle for efficient picking and retrieval.

### Impacted Value Scenario

- O2C

### Impacted Function

- Dispatch Process

### Expected Benefits

- Ease of picking  
- Better tracking and retrieval
